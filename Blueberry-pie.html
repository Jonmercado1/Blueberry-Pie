Blueberry Pie

Ingredients:

  • Pie Crust:

  • 2 1/2 cups flour, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp sugar, 1 cup chilled butter, and 1/4-1/2 cup ice water

  • Filling:

  • 4 cups fresh blueberries
  • 1/2-3/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp butter, diced
  • Egg Wash: 1 egg yolk mixed with 1 tsp wate

Directions

  • 1. Preheat Oven: 425°F (220°C).
  • 2. Filling: Toss blueberries with sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice, zest, cinnamon, and salt.
  • 3. Assemble Pie: Fill crust with berry mixture. Dot with butter. Cover with rolled-out dough for the top crust; seal edges. Cut slits for steam.
  • 4. Bake: Brush with egg wash. Bake at 425°F for 20 mins, then 350°F (175°C) for 30-35 mins until golden.
  • 5. Cool: Let cool before serving.
